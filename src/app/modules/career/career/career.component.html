Frequently Asked Questions

What is a coworking space?

A coworking space is a shared workspace in which people from different professional backgrounds work together. It gives you an amazing work environment and access to numerous state of the art amenities resulting in better communication, productivity, creativity and innovation.

Who uses coworking spaces?

From small startups to SMEs and large corporations, every type of organization can utilize a coworking space. In fact, individuals like business travelers, freelancers, remote workers, solo entrepreneurs and content creators etc. are also utilizing modern coworking spaces for their business needs.

How much does a coworking space cost?

The overall cost of a coworking space depends on the type of seats you choose- Hot Desks, Dedicated Desks, Private Cabins and the location where it is located.

What are the benefits of a coworking space?

A coworking space gives you an ample of work benefits such as enhanced productivity, invaluable networking opportunities, community events, enhanced creativity, good work-life balance, less operating expenses, more flexibility etc.

What are the amenities offered by coworking spaces?

Fast-speed wifi, ergonomic furniture, air conditioning, housekeeping service, support & maintenance, tight security, beverages, power backup, reception facilities, printer/scanner, washroom facilities, free tea/coffee, common area, meeting rooms, phone booths, recreation facilities, pantry, dedicated storage space and a lot more.

What types of flexible seats are available in coworking spaces?

There are hot desks, dedicated desks, private cabins and virtual offices. If you want a workspace for a day, then you can go with day passes. All the aforementioned flexible seat options are available as per your business requirements.
