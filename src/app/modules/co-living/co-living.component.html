Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the frequently asked questions that people enquired online before choosing a virtual office. We have gathered the most crucial ones and answer here to help you in making the right decision.

What is a coliving space?

Coliving Spaces relate to shared-housing to inhabit, work and have a good time. This experience includes fully furnished, bringing individuals together and creating a conducive environment for their interaction. A growing number of population across the globe are preferring coliving to unfasten benefits such as community, comfort and cost-saving.

Who utilises coliving spaces?

Coliving Spaces appeals particularly to the millennials due to rising property prices. These are opted by the people with the age group of 19 to 40 years.

How much do coliving spaces cost?

The costs of shared living spaces depends on the plan you choose- private room, double sharing rooms, triple sharing rooms or the studio apartments.

What are the advantages offered by coliving spaces?

From budget friendly spaces, state of the art amenities, networking opportunities to live a sustainable lifestyle, shared living spaces offer various advantages.

How long can one stay in coliving spaces?

One can stay either for a month, less than a year, to longer periods also.

Are coliving spaces safe to live in?

These are definitely a better option than living alone in a big and unknown city. There is provision of strict security and CCTV cameras. Unlike in a PG or flat system, the community aspect of coliving ensures ample security and socialization.

Is food provided in Coliving spaces?

It depends on the plan and the coliving you choose.
